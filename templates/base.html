Entropic Encounters

Exploring Inclusive Societies on Distant Worlds

Game Overview

Entropic Encounters is an immersive and collaborative game designed to explore the possibilities of building inclusive, resilient, and sustainable futures by using Science Fiction Thinking. Participants embark on an interstellar journey to learn from diverse, thriving societies on distant planets.

Learning Goals

  • Foster inclusivity by understanding how design can value and empower all individuals.
  • Promote equity by exploring solutions to systemic inequalities.
  • Encourage innovative thinking through futuristic and science fiction scenarios.
  • Connect fiction to reality by translating lessons into actionable strategies.
  • Understand the value of diversity in creating resilient societies.

Game Phases

  1. Starting the Mission: Build teams, decide roles (explorers and celestial citizens), and select planets or societies.
  2. The Encounter Begins: Explorers and celestial citizens start their interaction using monologues and guiding questions.
  3. Learning About the Society: Engage in discussions about the society's values, challenges, and achievements.
  4. Synthesis of Insights: Reflect and collaborate to develop key recommendations inspired by the exploration.

A desert world where inhabitants have mastered survival amidst heat and scarcity.

Hydros
